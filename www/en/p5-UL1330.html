  • FEP Wire
  • UL1330
  • Area of Application
  • UL1330 FEP Wire is suitable for fields that require high temperature endurance. Application ranges from household appliances, lighting devices, to industrial machines, and high-temperature furnaces. FEP is part of the fluoropolymer family, whose best known member is PTFE, or "Teflon®", which is a registered trademark of DuPond Co. Passed UL VW-1 Vertical fire test and multiple IEC tests, proven to be low smoke density, halogen free, and flame retardant.
  • Technical Data
  • Nominal Voltage: 600V AC
  • Temperature Range: -60°C~+200°C
  • OD Tolerance: ±0.1mm
  • Testing Voltage: 2500V
  • Conductor: Tinned Copper/ Silver Plated Copper
  • Insulator: FEP
  • Color: White, Black, Red, Yellow, Blue, Green, Brown, Yellow with Green stripes
